SnapBrowser Description

SnapBrowser is a free browsing assistant. Just point the camera at the text that you want to look up, and the app will show the search results in an in-app web-based browser.

FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS

  • Scan text with the camera for look up, no typing needed.
  • 14 different languages supported.
  • 6 different search engines to choose from.
  • Bookmark search queries that you want to save for later.
  • Show results in an in-app web-based browser or in your default browser app.
